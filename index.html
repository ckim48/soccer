Why Soccer Lineup Builder?

NLCS Soccer lineup builder is implemented to improve the performance and strategy of the team

Analyze the Team

'Soccer Lineup Builder' applied the FIFA (video game series) data. You can enter your teammates' abilities to analyze your players and the team.


Make Strategy

'Soccer Lineup Builder' recommends the best formation and strategy based on the ability of your players



Simulation

'Soccer Lineup Builder' will be used for the soccer tournament at the end of the year. We expect that the application will increase the win rate of the team
